my favorite food is leche flan

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 TABLESPOONS SUGAR
  • 230 GRAMS EDEN MELT SARAP, SLIGHTLY MELTED
  • 5 PIECES EGGS
  • 1 CAN EVAPORATED MILK
  • 1 CAN CONDENSE MILK
  • 1/2 CUP EDEN ORIGINAL, SHREDDED

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1 Put the sugar in a large llanera. Place over low heat until the sugar has melted and golden.

Step 2 Occasionally move the llanera to avoid burning and to distribute sugar evenly. Remove from heat and allow it to harden.

Step 3 In a bowl, beat Eden Melt Sarap until smooth. Add eggs one at a time. Make sure to beat the mixture after each egg is added.

Step 4 Add evaporated milk and condensed milk. Mix well. Once thoroughly mixed, let the mixture sit for a few minutes.

Step 5 Cover llanera with foil and place in a baking dish with 1-inch water. Bake for 375F for 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted into the leche flan comes out clean. Remove from oven and allow to cool.

Step 6 Turn leche flan over on a serving dish. Top with shredded Eden cheese and enjoy!
